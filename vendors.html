Frequently Asked Questions

What size is the vendor space?
Standard vendor spaces are approximately 10' x 10'. Larger spaces may be available upon request ‚Äî just note your needs in the application.
Do I need any permits?
Food vendors must have a valid Florida food service license or temporary event permit. We can provide guidance on obtaining the necessary permits for Sarasota County.
Is there parking for vendors?
Yes! Vendors receive reserved parking in a designated lot. ATVs will help transport your gear to the beach setup area.
What if it rains?
The event runs rain or shine. We'll provide weather updates leading up to the event. In case of severe weather cancellation, vendors will receive a full refund.
Can I bring my own tent/canopy?
Absolutely! We recommend a 10x10 pop-up canopy. You're responsible for your own setup, but we have volunteers to assist if needed. All structures must be weighted down properly for beach conditions.
How do I get my fee refunded if I can't make it?
Cancellations made 30+ days before the event receive a full refund. 14-30 days: 50% refund. Less than 14 days: no refund, but we'll try to find a replacement vendor and refund if successful.
