How It All Started

It started the way all great rugby ideas do: a few lads, a six-pack, and a beat-up ball on Siesta Key Beach.

What began as casual summer sessions to keep the Sarasota Rugby Club squad in shape quickly became something more. Every week, the group got bigger. New faces showed up ‚Äî some curious locals, some seasoned players, some just chasing the sunset (and the cooler). Before long, it wasn't just training. It was a ritual. A community.

Then someone asked the question: "We're on America's #1 beach... why don't we throw a proper tournament?"

And honestly? The logic was bulletproof: everyone loves rugby, everyone loves Siesta Key, and nobody's ever said no to a cold beer. So the crew ‚Äî Gary Jones, Ross Galbraith, RJ Dixon, Shaun Lulofs, Luke Paige, Jake Combe, and the rest of the Sarasota Rugby family ‚Äî decided to make it happen.
