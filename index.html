Welcome to Siesta 7s

Siesta 7s is the first annual beach rugby tournament hosted by Sarasota Rugby Club on the legendary white quartz sand of Siesta Key ‚Äî America's #1 beach. Expect high-energy matches, live music, great food, and a full weekend of beach community vibes.

Proudly Hosted By Sarasota Rugby Club Where rugby meets community, and teammates become family
Men's Team Youth Academy About the Club

On the Field

Competitive beach rugby 7s with teams battling it out on the sand. Multiple divisions, bracket-style tournament format, and awards for top teams.

Off the Field

Live music, local food vendors, cold drinks, and beach vibes. Whether you're playing or spectating, the atmosphere is electric all day long.

Full Weekend Experience

Kick off Friday at the pre-party, compete Saturday on the beach, then hit the Siesta Stumble bar crawl Saturday night. It's a full rugby weekend.
